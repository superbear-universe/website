## Basic Information
- **Name:** Dan
- **Age:** Late twenties
- **Sexuality:** Gay
- **Background:** Australian, currently travelling overseas
- **Occupation:** White collar office worker
- **Life Status:** Functional, restless, something missing he can't name
- **Transformation Type:** Corrupted Transformation (Brotherhood)
- **Narrative Role:** One-off main chapter character (for now — may be expanded in the future)

## Appearance

Dan is easy to like at a glance and easy to underestimate. His face is round and warm, soft-featured, with chubby cheeks and a slightly upturned nose that give him a boyishness he hasn't grown out of. His eyebrows are thick and brown and slightly too expressive for his own good.

- **Build:** Soft and chubby, carrying weight naturally without apology but without confidence either
- **Height:** Average
- **Hair:** Ginger-brown, longish, wavy and curly — falls where it wants to
- **Beard:** Medium length, scruffy, noticeably more ginger and auburn than his hair
- **Eyes:** Hazel-green, warm, quick to focus
- **Glasses:** Black rectangular frames, medium weight, simple and unpretentious
- **Skin:** Caucasian, warm-toned, faint natural tan

**Overall Impression:**
Dan looks like someone you'd trust immediately and forget to call back. Likeable, present, slightly unresolved.

## Canonical Outfit
Olive open overshirt worn loose and unbuttoned over a plain grey crew-neck t-shirt. Dark navy jeans. Brown leather lace-up boots, worn and practical. Nothing styled deliberately — clothes chosen for comfort, not impression.

## Personality

Dan is introverted by default but socially capable when the situation asks it of him. He can hold a conversation, make people laugh, show up when it counts. But new people cost him something. He gets shy, goes slightly quiet, second-guesses himself in ways he can't always hide. His social battery drains faster than most people would guess from looking at him.

He is not withdrawn. He is just someone who needs recovery time from the world.

- Warm and genuine in one-on-one settings
- Self-doubt surfaces most around strangers
- Dry humor, deployed quietly when he's comfortable
- Capable of real connection but slow to initiate it
- Notices more than he lets on

---

## Relationship to His Body

Dan's relationship with his body is defined by quiet dissatisfaction. Not hatred, not active distress — just the persistent low-level awareness that his body does not make him feel safe. He is soft where he wishes he were solid. He has never felt like someone who could defend himself, hold his ground, or take up space with any authority.

He has not done much about this. The desire for change is real but unfocused, without a clear direction to move in.

---

## Core Wound

Dan's defining wound is **powerlessness**.

Not a single incident necessarily — more a lifetime of feeling like the kind of person things happen to rather than the kind who can stop them. He is not aggressive, not bitter, not looking for revenge. He simply wants, quietly and persistently, to feel like he could handle something if it came for him.

That want has no clear outlet. He is overseas, working a job that doesn't ask much of him, single, restless in a way he can't fully articulate. Something is missing. He doesn't know what it looks like.

He just knows he hasn't found it yet.

---

## Core Themes
- Powerlessness as a way of life
- The desire for strength without a framework to find it
- Being far from home and further from yourself
- Wanting something you can't name until it's offered to you
